What we do

The Anvil Trust is an independent educational charity which runs Anvil Arts, the largest performing arts organisation in Hampshire. At The Anvil, The Haymarket, and The Forge, thousands of people from all over the region discover great performance – live.

Make a donation

We rely upon your contributions to support our work, as the income from ticket sales goes to the artists, not the venue. By making a contribution you are helping us to expand our programme of activities and benefit even more people in our community.
